How this score is built, and what it is not

The illustrative composite weighs three inputs actually held for every scored firm: estimated owner age (50%), ownership concentration (25%), and principal tenure (25%), all derived from SEC Form ADV and IAPD public records. Longitudinal behavioral extraction (headcount/AUM trend, office closures, ownership events) has run for a pilot subset and appears as dated evidence where available. Weights are judgment, not fitted parameters: the composite has not demonstrated predictive power in blind backtests to date.

Sources & limits

SEC Form ADV bulk filings and IAPD only: no FINRA BrokerCheck/U5, no scraped LinkedIn or paid databases. Ages are estimates from regulatory career records: “corroborated” means two independent anchors agree; “floor-only” means a minimum age is defensible but the band is not (typical of second-career principals). 12 of 123 firms lack sufficient public data and are flagged for manual review in the CSV export.
